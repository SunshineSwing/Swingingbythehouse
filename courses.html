Mondays at St.Croix-huset

Our courses take place on Monday evenings at St.Croix-huset in Fredrikstad. You dont need a partner to sign up, as we rotate partners.

In the beginners-course, no prior knowledge is required. We will focus on the basic steps, figures and leading/ following technique. We will be learning the core basic skills and rhythm of the dance so that you will be able to start dancing socially. Level 1 consists of six classes on monday evenings from 19:30-21:00. The price is 900,- for the whole course, 700,- for students, and 1500,- if you sign up as a couple. We will arrange a social event to enjoy what we have learned in the end - this will be fun!
