About Us

Swinging by The House was founded in 2020, and is organized and run by Mary, Christoffer, and Sara.

The Team

MARY

Dance teacher

Mary has been dancing her whole life. She got introduced to the world of swing in 2017 when she started dancing Boogie Woogie and Authentic Jazz, and she immediateley fell in love with it. The semester that followed, she began to learn Lindy Hop, Collegiate Shag, St. Louis Shag, and Balboa. She has been teaching classes and tasters in Bårdar Swing Club for a year. Together with Christoffer, she is travelling all across Europe, constantly enriching her knowledge about swing dances and trying to perfectionate her technique and musicality.

CHRISTOFFER

Dance teacher

Christoffer started with swing dancing almost 5 years ago. His first dance was Lindy hop, and he immediately became a big fan. Half a year later he branched out into Authentic jazz and Collegiate Shag, eventually also picking up Balboa, St. louis Shag and Boogie Woogie. He started attending international workshops across Europe trying to increase his repertoire and develop his technique. He started teaching at Bårdar Swing Club a year ago and he has not stopped ever since.

SARA

Founder

Sara loves dancing, and have been dancing contemporary most of her life. She was introduced to Lindy Hop as a student in Denmark, and fell in love with the music, the movements, and the warm and social environment. To learn Lindy Hop made her happy, and when she moved back to Norway she wanted to bring this environment with her. She was happy to find Mary and Christoffer, and started Swinging by The House together with them.
