Events

We organize different events during the year!

Hello Lindy Hop!

Every semester we are organizing an event called "Hello Lindy Hop" to introduce the dance to people who wants to try it out! Normally it takes place in january and august. Its free, and you dont need to bring a partner!

We start with a intro-course in Lindy Hop followed by a open social dance to try out what you have learned! You can ask the teachers about the following courses.

“Once you got the Lindy bug, there`s no way to stop!"

Weekend workshops!

We organized some workshops in other swing dances as well, like Shag, and Boogie Woogie, or solo jazz. They are all in the swing-family together with Lindy Hop.

Social Dancing at Tæps!

We have organized some social dancing at the local bar Tæps in Fredrikstad. We dance, we have something to drink, and practice what we´ve learned!

Digital workshops!

During the time of Corona we have been organizing digital live-stream workshops to keep the dancing going from home.
